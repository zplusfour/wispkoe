Terms of Service

Wispkoe has been, is, and always will be a place where you can freely speak your mind. However, to help keep this a safe yet still great community, we have to put some rules in place.

1. Takedowns

Takedowns of posts can only be done by the admin. If you wish to send a post takedown request, please email getdevved@gmail.com, give a screenshot of the post and give the reason for takedown request. It shall be looked into.

2. Misconducts

Here are the base rules for Wispkoe:

  • Using Wispkoe to promote illegal goods or behaviour is strictly prohibited, no matter the level of illegality.
  • Trying to promote sexual content in any case is prohibited.
  • Spam posting (repeatedly posting in large amounts) is prohibited.
  • Intentionally trying to XSS the site or place viruses is not allowed in any case.
  • Blackmail, extortion, anything like that is not allowed.
  • Impersonating admin (or anyone) is not allowed.

    • These are subject to change with or without notification. Failure to follow them will result in post takedowns. If rules are broken repeatedly, you may be banned.

3. Privacy

Wispkoe does not collect any personal/sensitive info (name, age, date, birth, etc.) and any data you post on this site is not our problem and does not count as data collection.
